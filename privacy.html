Last Updated: June 2026

At Astro Insight, we hold your cosmic identity and data privacy with utmost reverence. This policy outlines exactly how we handle the planetary alignments and personal information you entrust to our care when you visit our website (shandorv.github.io/Oracle).

1. Information We Collect

To deliver accurate readings and premium PDF projections, we gather basic indicators:

  • Account Data: Email address and username when you interface with our systems.
  • Celestial Coordinates: Date, exact time, and general location of your birth. This is exclusively used to calculate natal charts and planetary degrees.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your charts are processed mathematically via our internal engines. We will use your information to:

  • Generate and securely mail your requested personalized PDF reports.
  • Maintain your Personal Grimoire log across local sessions.
  • Send critical technical or transactional alerts regarding billing cycles.

3. Third-Party Payment Protection

Financial alignment transactions (such as purchasing premium reports) are managed completely outside our architecture. We route processing through verified secure processors (Stripe or PayPal). Astro Insight never reads, stores, or handles raw credit card data or banking protocols.

4. Cookies and Advertising (Google AdSense)

We deploy basic, lightweight analytic tags to monitor generalized visitor metrics. These metrics never target individual charts or identify unique souls; they merely guide us on improving interface elements.

Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising: We partner with Google and other third-party vendors to serve ads when you visit our site. Google uses cookies (such as the DART cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other sites on the Internet. These advertising networks track your generalized browsing habits to display relevant, targeted advertisements.

You may opt out of personalized advertising at any time by visiting Google Ads Settings or by configuring your browser to decline all third-party tracking cookies.

5. International Data Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you possess celestial rights over your mortal digital presence under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the EU, and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) in the US:

  • GDPR (EU Users): You have the right to access, rectify, or demand the absolute erasure of your personal data from our databases. You can object to data processing or request data portability.
  • CCPA (California Users): You have the right to know what personal data we collect, request its deletion, and opt-out of the sale of your personal information (Please note: we do not sell your personal data).

To invoke any of these digital rights, please submit a request to our support email listed below.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for a general audience and are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If a child under the legal age provides us with personal details without verifiable parental consent, we will delete that information from our servers immediately.

7. Data Retention

We only keep your personal information for as long as your account is active, or as long as necessary to provide you with our services. If you choose to delete your data, it will be wiped from our databases within 30 days, except where required for standard tax accounting histories.
