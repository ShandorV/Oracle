Astrology & Forecasts

How often are the horoscopes updated?

Our cosmic database updates dynamically to reflect real-time alignments. General transits and daily metrics refresh precisely at midnight based on your system's current time zone.

Can I save a specific reading for later review?

Yes. By clicking the "Inscribe to Grimoire" button inside any active modal reading, the data snapshot is preserved forever in your local browser history log beneath the Main Oracle dashboard.

Premium PDF Projections

What do I receive when I order a personalized report?

You will receive a beautifully formatted, comprehensive digital manuscript containing deep birth chart assessments, current multi-month progressive transits, and targeted life-path analyses mapped to your exact birth minutes.

How long does it take for my PDF document to arrive?

Our generation engine builds your document automatically following successful confirmation. Most documents materialize directly in your email inbox within 5 to 15 minutes.

Billing & Access

Are my online financial transactions safe?

Absolutely. All payment handling is sandboxed securely through enterprise partners Stripe and PayPal. Astro Daily never views or records your raw payment keys or account numbers.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You have total autonomy over your membership. You can cancel your subscription with a single click at any time through the Billing tab in your account profile. You will maintain full access to your premium features until the exact end of your current billing cycle.

Troubleshooting

Why am I not receiving my daily horoscope email?

If our emails aren't reaching your inbox, please try the following steps:

  • Check your Spam, Promotions, or Updates folder.
  • Add support@astrodaily.com to your safe senders list or email address book.
  • Verify that your email address is spelled correctly under your account configurations.
